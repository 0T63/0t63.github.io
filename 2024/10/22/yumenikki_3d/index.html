游戏简介

**注意 本文讲的梦日记3d为同人制作 并非原作者KIKIYAMA，本3d作品的原作者为ZykovEddy**

Yume Nikki 3d 是一款基于 Yume Nikki宇宙的粉丝游戏（如果你还没玩过原版的 Yume Nikki，我强烈推荐它）。该游戏不是简单的 3d 重制版，而是原版的衍生产品，有很多额外的内容。我没有使用 RPG Maker，而是使用了 90 年代的旧 3D 引擎。

请注意，无需成为原版游戏的粉丝即可享受 YN3D。这个概念很容易理解，而且很容易上手和玩。

Note that it’s not necessary to be a fan of the original game to enjoy YN3D.
就算不太喜欢原作的人也可以尝试一下本作。
The concept is understandable and it’s easy to pick up and play.
游戏难度较低，很容易上手。

You play as a little girl Madotsuki.
主角是名为附窗子的少女。
Your task is to find six “effects” and three keys, which are hidden in a dream world.
游戏目的是收集散落于梦境世界的六个“效果”和三把钥匙。
The game starts in your room.
游戏开始时，主角身处自己的房间。
You can use the bed to enter the dream world or desk to save your game.
可以躺上床进入梦境，或者在书桌前存档。

Most locations in Dream world are connected by series of portals and for the most part I tried to create my own locations instead of copying the ones from the original game.
梦中的各个地图由传送点连接。地图并不完全和原作相仿，有些是二次创作。
Only a few locations have references to the original Yume Nikki, such as Neon Tile Path or Poniko’s house (yes, this game has a full recreation of Uboa event in 3d).
只有一小部份地图，如霓虹道路、浅濑部屋（包括3D版关灯变脸事件）是完全模仿《梦日记》原作的。
Some of the effects are requied to progress in few areas. The most important effects, such as bicycle and the knife, are hidden in the locations connected to Nexus (the hub location).
自行车和菜刀等最重要的效果所在的地图，从门的世界可以直接到达；但少部份效果需要传送数张地图之后才能取得。

Isolated DLC is a little side story, based on Yume Nikki 3D.
“Isolated”是独立于游戏本篇的特典。
The reason why I decided to release it as DLC and not to include it in upcoming v 0.03 is because I wanted to experiment with Yume Nikki concept a little bit.
作者尝试在发布本篇的0.03版前，用不同的方式来表现梦日记的氛围。
The gameplay is different from the original game, and all locations are brand new.
游戏方式和本篇不同，地图皆为作者原创。
Extra graphics and music was made exclusively for this DLC.
这个特典里包含本篇所没有的图片和BGM。

Enjoy!
